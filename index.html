My UX Skills

User research

User research involves gathering insights and understanding the behaviors, needs, and motivations of users through various methods like interviews, surveys, and observations. It serves as the foundation for designing user-centric products by providing critical data to inform design decisions.

User experience

User Experience (UX) focuses on enhancing user satisfaction by improving the usability, accessibility, and pleasure provided in the interaction with a product. It encompasses all aspects of the end-user's interaction, including the interface, graphics, industrial design, physical interaction, and manual.

User interface design

User Interface (UI) design is the process of creating interfaces in software or computerized devices with a focus on looks or style. The goal is to make the user's interaction as simple and efficient as possible, through elements like buttons, icons, and other visual elements.
